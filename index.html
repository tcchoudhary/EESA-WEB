WELCOME TO EESA

EESA (Event Equipment Services Association) is a not for profit organization. This association is open to all companies in India having invested in their own equipment & offers equipment rental and their professional services for the event industry

This Association covers Professional event equipment rental companies the likes of which include Sound, Stage Lighting, LED & Video Displays, Truss & Rigging, Stage, Infrastructure, Decor & Floral, Generators, General Lighting, SFX, Lasers & Pyro.

WAY JOIN EESA ?

COMMUNITY

Help grow together with opportunities, overcome challenges and address industry issues.

ADVOCACY

As an association, our agenda is to influence decision makers in government, business and industry.

GROWTH

Members value being part of a community because of the opportunities to learn and develop their long-term careers.

Insights

Learn the trends, gather data on the industry’s contribution to the economy, know the Power of Exhibitions as both a driver of the economy and an effective marketing and engagement channel.
